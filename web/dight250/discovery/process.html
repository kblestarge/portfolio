The Process

Step-by-step instructions:

Step 1

Pour water on the rocks to create steam which increases the temperture.

Step 2

Spill a few drops of eucalyptus oil on the rocks to bring out the flavor.

Step 3

Be liberal when drinking water in case of dehydration from sweat.

Step 4

Stay in sauna for 15 minutes repeating previous steps as needed.

Step 5

Take ice-cold shower for 5 minutes. Repeat process at least twice.
