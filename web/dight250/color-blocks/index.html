Color Blocks

Representing Star Wars characters

Darth Vader

His cape is black. His mask is black. His sole is black. His lightsaber, however, is red.

Yoda

He represents organic side of the Jedi with his natural greens and blue lightsaber.

Chewbacca

He has many shades of brown in his hair and sometimes carries a grey crossbow.

Leia Organa

She is a white princess from the planet called Alderaan. Her hair is dark brown.
