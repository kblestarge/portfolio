About us

Michael O’Day – CEO & Founder

Michael is a social entrepreneur with 8 years of humanitarian experience in Madagascar. He founded a non-profit, the Madagascar Cooperative Foundation, back in 2011 and has acted as its country director for the last 4 years.

Georges Ramiadamanana – COO & Founder

George has a Bachelor’s in Business and a Master’s in Marketing. Has 30 years experience in business and has owned and operated his own a real estate firm, Immoblier Services, in the Capitol of Madagascar since 2003.

Lala Andrianarivo – CFO & Founder

Certified Notary and Accountant with a Master’s in Economics. Runs a successful business in Madagascar, appraising real estate and legally transferring land titles for transactions involving the sale of land.

Richard Herinjatovo – Chief Architect & Founder

Richard has over 20 years of experience as an architect. He received a Bachelor degree in Architecture at the National Institute of Infrastructure in Madagascar. He is the head architect for the government of Madagascar’s affordable housing program, which provides homes to government employees.

Eva Rakotoarimanana – Chief Credit Officer & Founder

Eva received her degree in Business Management in 1997. She went on to receive advanced certificates in both Business and English. After finishing her education in 2003 she took a job as a banker at BFV-Société Générale, an international French bank, and one of the largest in Madagascar. Since 2009 she has been a director of one of BFV’s busiest branches located in the downtown of Madagascar’s Capitol.
