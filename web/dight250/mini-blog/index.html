POTENTIAL THREATS

Answer: Digital Humanities is the process of turning humans into cyborgs (cybernetic organism).

While this transformation process leads to many break-thoughs in technology, it also introduces a serious problem. As more and more people turn into cyborgs, artificial intelligence (AI) will become the dominant form of intelligence on Earth, with computers or robots effectively taking control of the planet away from the human race, and posing an existential risk which could wipe all people out.
